Ingredients

Inner Ice Cream Layer
  • 1 part frozen blueberry purée (for colour and flavour)
  • 1 part high-quality Greek yogurt or coconut cream
  • 1/2 part vanilla whey protein concentrate powder
  • Sweetener to taste: 1/4 part date paste or agave nectar
Outer Chocolate Layer
  • High cacao dark chocolate (70% or higher, lower sugar)
  • Coconut oil (to ensure a crisp, smooth coating)

Recipe (DIY)

The process involves homogenizing the blueberry purée, Greek yogurt/cream base, whey protein, and sweeteners, flash-freezing the mix into a stable core, and then enrobing the core in a temperature-controlled dark chocolate and coconut oil compound coating.
